Mumbai News Live:Sachin Vaze produced before Mumbai sessions court by the NIA
Mumbai, Thane, and Kalyan Dombivli regions continue to report a massive spike in new COVID-19 cases. For the fourth day consecutive day, Mumbai reported over 1,500 new cases. With the addition of 1,708 on Saturday, the overall tally has increased to 3,41,985. 941 people also recovered during the day, while five more succumbed to the virus. Thane reported over 300 new cases, while Kalyan Dombivli over 400. Overall, Maharashtra recorded 15,602 fresh COVID-19 cases and 88 deaths, taking the tally to 22,97,793 and the toll to 52,811. Maharashtra is now left with 1,18,525 active cases.
